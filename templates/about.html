Biography

Thawng Hmung was born in Burma/Myanmar. At a young age, he moved to Malaysia due to the instability of his home country. After about a year in Malaysia, through the United Nations Refugee Agency, he left for the United States as a refugee. The first home in the States was in Chicago, but after only a few days, they moved to a Battle Creek, a small town in Michigan. Which to this day, call it home. He graduated from Lakeview High School in the year 2020. He is currently attending Michigan Technological University with a major in Computer Science and a minor in Mathematical Science.
